About us

Who we are

We are a consulting ministry using technology to reach a world in desperate need of the good news of Jesus Christ.

What we do

For 13 years, we have focused on assisting the global church to be disciples of Jesus Christ by providing tools and needed training.

How we do it

We work with the global church, using an open-access platform to create, translate, and distribute open discipleship resources in any language.

Why we do it

We are compelled to say among the nations, “the Lord reigns!” There has never been a more critical time to tell the world of God’s Glory.
