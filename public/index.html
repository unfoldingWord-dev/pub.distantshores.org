Who we are

We are a consulting ministry using technology to reach a world in desperate need of the good news of Jesus Christ.

What we do

For 13 years, we have focused on assisting the global church to be disciples of Jesus Christ by providing tools and needed training.

How we do it

We work with the global church, using an open-access platform to create, translate, and distribute open discipleship resources in any language.

Why we do it

We are compelled to say among the nations, “the Lord reigns!” There has never been a more critical time to tell the world of God’s Glory.

Is it possible to end the spiritual famine of the global church?

We think so! Hundreds of millions of believers all over the world are in immense need of discipleship resources and theological training, but God has given His Church new opportunities and new tools to meet this need. There is, however, a massive obstacle preventing us from taking full advantage of these new opportunities. Find out about the need, the opportunities to meet that need, the obstacle that hinders, and a solution to the problem in this brand new book about world missions in the 21st century: The Christian Commons.
