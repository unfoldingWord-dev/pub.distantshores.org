About the Author

Tim Jore is the Executive Director of Distant Shores Media. He works with the Distant Shores Media team to develop culturally-appropriate discipleship materials and technology solutions that enable the global church to equip themselves for spiritual growth, using the technology available to them. Prior to joining Distant Shores Media, Tim spent ten years in various aspects of Bible translation, serving in Papua New Guinea, Brazil, and Southeast Asia.

Watch a presentation by Tim at the Mobile Ministry Forum Consultation that addresses many of the topics covered in The Christian Commons: "Faster. Farther. Cheaper. Better. The unrestricted, mobile future of the global church."
